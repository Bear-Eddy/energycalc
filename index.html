üìä To Put This in Perspective:

1 hour of mixed AI use (mostly text, some images):

‚Ä¢ ~0.5-1.5 kWh total energy consumption

‚Ä¢ Equivalent to running a microwave for 30-90 minutes

‚Ä¢ 10x more than a Google search, but 5x less than streaming HD video

Heavy AI user (4+ hours daily with frequent image generation):

‚Ä¢ ~3-5 kWh/day - similar to running a space heater for 2-3 hours

Factors That Affect AI Energy Use:

Model Size

GPT-4 uses ~10x more energy than GPT-3.5 for the same query

Query Length

Longer conversations and context windows increase energy linearly

Server Location

Data centers powered by renewable energy have lower carbon impact

Time of Day

Off-peak usage when grids are cleaner reduces carbon footprint

Task Complexity

Multi-modal tasks (text + image) compound energy requirements

Provider Efficiency

OpenAI, Google, and Anthropic have different infrastructure efficiency

üí° Energy-Saving Tips for AI Users:

‚Ä¢ Batch similar queries together rather than multiple separate sessions

‚Ä¢ Download and reuse generated images instead of regenerating

‚Ä¢ Consider whether you really need that AI-generated video

‚Ä¢ Use AI during off-peak hours when grids are cleaner (nighttime)
